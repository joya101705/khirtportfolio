MyPerformance : The Ultimate Workout and Nutritionist for Exercise Enthusiasts.

The Ultimate Workout Scheduler and Nutritionist for Exercise Enthusiasts offers a robust and intuitive platform for users to effectively workout routine plan, advisable nutrition and gives accurate workout for all.

MyPerformance

IT112 subject 1st Sem

Functionality: The “MyPerformance” designed to all Exercise Enthusiasts to help Exercise enthusiasts to improve their daily health lifestyle. The function of our System below:
1. The Exercise Enthusiast will give their personal information such as First name, Middle Initials, Last name, age, height and Weight
2. Note 16 years old below can’t avail this program
3. The Exercise Enthusiast have a 3 choices (Full Body, Upper Body and Lower Body) and then the System will give output to exercise enthusiast that choose your skill level such as (Beginner, Intermediate and advanced)
4. The System will provide Workout plan base on their Age, What part of the body want user to improve and their skill level
5. After the Workout plan, system will ask the Exercise Enthusiast if he needs help regarding to his/her nutrition
6. And the system will provide nutrition plan for Exercise Enthusiast
