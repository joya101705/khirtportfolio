ABOUT

For our IT121 Comprog final exam we were task to do a crud management system with a group of 3 max members and we decided to do Cybercafes, also known as Internet Cafe, are establishments that provide public access to the internet, as well as other computer-related services. They are a popular form of internet access in areas where home internet connections are not widely available or affordable. It provides customers with access to computers, and sometimes even food and beverages. We provide a convenient and affordable way for people to access the internet, print documents, and even play online games.
